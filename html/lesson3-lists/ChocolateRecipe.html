How to Make Chocolate Milk

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour the milk into a cup.
  2. Add the cocoa powder and sugar.
  3. Stir until everything is mixed well.
  4. Add whipped cream on top if you like.
  5. Enjoy your delicious chocolate milk!
