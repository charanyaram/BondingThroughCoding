My Favorite Color: Blue

Blue and pink are my favorite colors! Blue is the color of the sky on a sunny day and the big, deep ocean, while pink reminds me of beautiful sunsets and blooming flowers.

I love blue because it makes me feel calm and happy, and pink because it’s bright and full of joy. Did you know that blue is also the color of superheroes who protect the world? It reminds me of feeling brave and strong, just like them! Pink, on the other hand, feels playful and loving, like the color of cotton candy at a fun fair.

A Fun Story About Blue

One day, I was painting a picture of a beach during sunset. I ran out of blue paint for the ocean and pink for the sky! So, I had to mix other colors to make my own shades of blue and pink. It was so much fun! My picture turned out amazing, and everyone thought the beach looked magical. That’s why I love blue and pink—they’re magical, peaceful, joyful, and full of surprises!
